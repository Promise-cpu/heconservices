Registration Requirements

For Business Name Registration

  • Two proposed business names (in order of preference)
  • Nature of business activities
  • Business address
  • Passport photograph of proprietor/partners
  • Valid means of identification (International Passport, Driver's License, or National ID Card)
  • Utility bill as proof of address (not more than 3 months old)

For Private Limited Company

  • Two proposed company names (in order of preference)
  • Nature of business activities
  • Registered office address
  • Details of shareholders (minimum of 2, maximum of 50)
  • Details of directors (minimum of 2)
  • Company secretary details (can be appointed later)
  • Authorized share capital
  • Passport photographs and valid ID of all shareholders and directors

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does CAC registration take?

Business Name registration typically takes 1-2 weeks, while Private Limited Company registration takes 2-4 weeks, depending on CAC processing times and document completeness.

What is the difference between Business Name and Company registration?

A Business Name registration is for sole proprietors or partnerships, offering no separation between personal and business liabilities. A Private Limited Company is a separate legal entity, providing limited liability protection to its shareholders.

Can I register my company without a physical office address?

Yes, you can use a virtual office address for registration. We can assist you in obtaining a credible virtual office address that meets CAC requirements for business registration.

What happens after my company is registered?

After registration, you'll receive your Certificate of Incorporation. You'll then need to open a corporate bank account, register for taxes, and ensure ongoing compliance with filing annual returns and other statutory requirements.
